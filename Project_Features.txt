====================================================================
               MODERN BARCODE BILLING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
====================================================================

OVERVIEW:
---------
This project is a full-stack, web-based billing application designed 
for retail stores or shops (specifically optimized for weight-based 
pricing, like spices or loose goods). It uses modern web technologies 
to allow shopkeepers to manage products, automatically generate 1D 
barcodes, scan products via webcam, and manage sales dashboards.

TECHNOLOGY STACK:
-----------------
* Frontend: React.js (Vite), CSS3, html5-qrcode (Scanner), react-barcode
* Backend: Node.js, Express.js
* Database: MongoDB
* Communication: Axios REST API

====================================================================
                        CORE FEATURES
====================================================================

1. ADMIN PRODUCT MANAGEMENT (CRUD)
   - Add Products: Input product name, price (per 50g), and a custom barcode ID (or auto-generate one).
   - Automatic Barcode Generation: The system instantly generates a standard 1D CODE-128 barcode for the product.
   - View Products: A clean table displays all added products along with their visual barcode.
   - Edit/Delete: Easily modify product pricing or remove outdated items from the database.

2. WEBCAM BARCODE SCANNER
   - Real-Time Scanning: Uses the device's webcam to scan 1D barcodes instantly.
   - Highly Optimized: The scanner is restricted to CODE-128 formats and uses the full screen feed, ensuring lightning-fast recognition even from mobile screens.
   - Auto-Add: Scanning a barcode automatically fetches the product from the database and adds it to the active bill.

3. MANUAL BILLING OVERRIDE
   - If the scanner is broken or a barcode is missing, users can manually select products from a dynamic dropdown list populated directly from the database.

4. DYNAMIC CART & INVOICING
   - Weight Calculation: Enter weight in either Grams (g) or Kilograms (kg). The system automatically calculates the price based on the base rate (price per 50g).
   - Invoice Generation: Generates a clean, professional, print-ready invoice showing the date, unique invoice number, itemized list, and grand total.

5. SALES & BILLS DASHBOARD
   - Persistent Records: Every finalized bill is saved to the MongoDB database.
   - Monthly Aggregation: Bills are automatically grouped by Month & Year (e.g., "May 2026").
   - Grand Totals: The system calculates and displays the total revenue generated for each specific month.
   - Itemized Viewing: A "View" button on any past bill opens a modal showing exactly what was sold, weights, and rates.
   - Record Deletion: Admins can delete faulty or test bills, which instantly updates the monthly grand total.

====================================================================
                        HOW IT WORKS (WORKFLOW)
====================================================================

STEP 1: ADDING INVENTORY
- The Admin navigates to the "Admin" -> "Products Management" tab.
- They enter a new product (e.g., "Premium Coffee Beans", $5.00 per 50g).
- They click "Save Product". The system generates a barcode (e.g., P001).
- The shopkeeper can print this barcode and stick it on the coffee bin.

STEP 2: SCANNING & BILLING A CUSTOMER
- The cashier navigates to the "Billing" tab.
- They click "Start Scanner" and hold the customer's item (with the barcode) up to the webcam.
- The scanner reads "P001" and instantly adds "Premium Coffee Beans" to the cart.
- The cashier weighs the beans (e.g., 250g) and enters "250" into the cart's weight input. 
- The system automatically calculates the price (5 * 5 = $25.00).

STEP 3: GENERATING THE INVOICE
- The cashier clicks "Generate Final Bill".
- The system saves the bill to the database and displays a clean, printable Invoice.
- The cashier can print it for the customer and click "New Bill" for the next person in line.

STEP 4: REVIEWING SALES
- At the end of the month, the owner checks the "Admin" -> "Bills & Sales" tab.
- They instantly see "May 2026: GRAND TOTAL 2800.00".
- They can browse the list of bills to see exactly what items sold on which days.
